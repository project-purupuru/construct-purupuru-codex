# Jani

The Purupuru mascot — a dual-nature being. Publicly known as a cute rounded bear character, but secretly an interdimensional entity whose true form was glimpsed in ancient cave paintings. Jani holds knowledge of the Puru Cult and serves as a bridge between the mundane world and the deeper honey-magic of Tsuheji.

## Duality

- **Public face**: Adorable mascot, rounded and approachable
- **Hidden nature**: Interdimensional entity, origin through deep honey visions
