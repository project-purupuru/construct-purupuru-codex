# The Unearthing (Hakkutsu-ba)

*Name origin: hakkutsu-ba -- excavation site*

On the eastern outskirts of Horai. A terraced hillside scattered with broken clay pots marks the site where the Puruhani were first dug up. Now a quiet public park -- fragments of ancient vessels still embedded in exposed earth, roped off behind wooden fences. A modest shrine at the center honors the Puruhani; locals leave small jars of honey as offerings. On certain nights, the clay shards are said to hum.
