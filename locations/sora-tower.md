# Sora Tower

*Name origin: sora -- sky*

A massive self-sustaining architectural and ecological marvel that serves as the capital of the city. Beautiful gardens line many tiers, great vines hang down for many stories where residents harvest fresh produce from their balconies. Powered by vast solar arrays. The Sky-eyes Dome observatory sits at its peak.

## Associated Locations

The tower anchors two nearby destinations: The Golden Veil's apiaries occupy the lower tiers, while Gateway Cafe is positioned at ground level.
