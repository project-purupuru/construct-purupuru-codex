# Konka Market

*Name origin: mixed fruit*

"Mixed fruit" market. Everything from fresh fruit to the latest hybrids grown by local arborists. Rows of mismatched stalls overflow with produce that shouldn't exist -- blue melons, honey-veined peaches, flowers that smell different to every person.
