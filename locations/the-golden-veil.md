# The Golden Veil (Kinsha)

*Name origin: Kinsha -- golden gauze/veil*

An entire quarter where the honey industry lives. Terraced apiaries climb Sora Tower's lower levels, thousands of hives humming in unison. On warm days the air shimmers gold with pollen and light. Honey merchants, mead brewers, wax artisans, and royal jelly bathhouses line narrow amber-lit corridors. The oldest families trace beekeeping lineage to the city's founding.
