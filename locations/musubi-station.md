# Musubi Station

*Name origin: musubi -- connection, tying together*

Horai's central hub. Ancient timber framework meets glass and steel canopies. Dozens of platforms across multiple levels. The deepest levels brush against Old Horai -- late-night commuters swear the oldest platform leads somewhere not on any map. A honeycomb-patterned departure board dominates the main hall. The station's signature chime is said to be the oldest recorded melody in Tsuheji.

Connects even the furthest reaches of the city and the broader continent.
