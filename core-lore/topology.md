# World Topology — Tsuheji's Places

> "Each place does one thing. Composition through pipes. The pipes are invisible. The places are warm."

## Architecture

The world operates as an ECS architecture where surfaces are Entities (disposable), convex elements are Components (truth), and systems operate independently. UNIX philosophy: small, composable units. TASTE: materials possess substance, honey as the accent.

## Spatial Grammar

Progressive identity deepens from anonymous through element-aware to wallet-linked, with no restrictive gates. The worldIdentity accumulates: sessionId, element, avatar, walletAddress, discoveredAt, lastSeenAt.
