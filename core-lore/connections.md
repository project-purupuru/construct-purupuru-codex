# Key Connections

The narrative threads that bind the Purupuru universe together.

## OBB → Puruhani → Tsuheji

The Ooga Booga Beras fabricated Puruhani from honey. Puruhani transitioned to Tsuheji, cultivated societal development, subsequently hibernated. Recent excavation has restored them.

## Deep Honey → Cave Paintings → Jani

Early caretakers ingested Puruhani vessel honey, experienced OBB world visions, and rendered the Cave Bera. Accumulated cultural resonance — strengthened via honey — transformed imagery into sentient interdimensional consciousness: Jani.

## Jani → Multiverse

Jani represents singular consciousness expressed distinctly across dimensional boundaries. Within Tsuheji, widespread perception treats him as commercial mascot iconography. The Puru cult recognizes underlying reality. He communicates selectively but remains cryptic.

## HENLO → Everything

The five-character framework (H-E-N-L-O) structures personality pairings, elemental assignments, ethical concepts, chromatic language, and bear classifications. This constitutes the organizational foundation underlying all character development, Elemental Jani expressions, and the comprehensive color language.

## Caretaker ↔ Puruhani

All pairings are mutualistic. The bond is called KIZUNA (絆 = bond). Each pair embodies one of the five Confucian virtues through their dynamic — not through direct action, but through what their relationship reveals about virtue.
