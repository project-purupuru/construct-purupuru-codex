# Horai — The Main City

**Horai** (蜂来, "where bees gather" — also echoing 蓬莱, the mythical paradise of East Asian legend) is the main city on Tsuheji. Known for its history as a gathering place of ideas and its dedication to ecology. A paradise of green and gold.

## Structure

- **Sora Tower**: Central landmark. A massive self-sustaining architectural and ecological marvel that serves as the capital of the city. Beautiful gardens line many tiers, great vines hang down for many stories where residents harvest fresh produce from their balconies. Powered by vast solar arrays. The **Sky-eyes Dome** observatory sits at its peak.
- **Honeycomb Districts**: Neighborhoods built in the same honeycomb shape as the bees that produced the honey they treasure. This structure persisted as the city grew, creating distinct districts and even influencing the architecture of skyscrapers.
- **Train Systems**: Elaborate networks run through Old Horai underground and the surface, connecting even the furthest reaches of the city and the broader continent. The central hub is **Musubi Station** (結び, connection/tying together).
- **Ship of Theseus Architecture**: Old buildings are NEVER demolished. Conservation and preservation are pushed to the absolute limit. Historical importance and tradition come before necessity. Buildings are preserved as intensely as possible, or renovated so many times they take on an unrecognizable form. A wooden shrine gets rebuilt in steel but keeps its exact original form. Wood-and-tile houses stand alongside vast concrete apartment complexes.
