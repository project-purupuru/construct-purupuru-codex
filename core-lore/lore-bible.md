# Project Purupuru — Canonical Lore Bible

> Status: Active — this is the single source of truth for Project Purupuru lore.
> Authority: @gumi. Changes to canon require lore authority approval.

## 1. The World of Tsuheji

**Tsuheji** (土和吉) is the fictional continent where everything in Project Purupuru takes place. Name derived from tsuchi (土, soil), he/wa (和, harmony/peace), and ji (吉, lucky).

### World Constants

- **Aesthetic**: Ghibli-inspired. Warm, painterly, inviting.
- **Tone**: Positive, wholesome, low-conflict. Wholesome escapism.
- **Magic System**: All magic rooted in **honey**. Sentient honey creatures (Puruhani), honeycomb architecture, bees everywhere. No dragons, unicorns, or traditional fantasy.
- **Henlo**: The primary cultural constant. A greeting, lifestyle, ethos, and for some, a belief system. The one unifying thread.
- **Technology Level**: Slightly utopian, strong emphasis on sustainability and ecological living.

## 2. Horai — The Main City

**Horai** (蜂来, "where bees gather" — echoing 蓬莱, mythical paradise). Known for its dedication to ecology. A paradise of green and gold.

- **Sora Tower**: Central landmark. Self-sustaining architectural/ecological marvel. Sky-eyes Dome observatory at peak.
- **Honeycomb Districts**: Neighborhoods built in honeycomb shape.
- **Train Systems**: Through Old Horai underground and surface. Central hub: Musubi Station (結び, connection).
- **Ship of Theseus Architecture**: Old buildings NEVER demolished. Conservation pushed to absolute limit. Old designs rebuilt in modern materials keeping exact original form.

## 3. Henlo

The greeting, the ethos, the way of life. Five letters, five elements, five characters.

| Letter | Element | Character | Trait | Puruhani Trait |
|--------|---------|-----------|-------|----------------|
| H | Wood | Kaori | Hopeful | Happy |
| E | Earth | Nemu | Empty | Exhausted |
| N | Fire | Akane | Naughty | Nefarious |
| L | Metal | Ren | Loyal | Loving |
| O | Water | Ruan | Overstimulated | Overwhelmed |

## 4. Puruhani

Five sentient honey creatures created by the Ooga Booga Beras. Soft, golden, honey-colored blobs that drape sleepily over clay pots. Each has a distinct element, personality trait, and pot color. Individual names remain unknown (GAP-002).

## 5. The Caretakers (KIZUNA Generation)

Five young people who discovered and bonded with the Puruhani. Each forms a mutualistic pair with their Puruhani partner.

## 7. Jani

Dual-nature being: public mascot (cute rounded bear) and hidden interdimensional entity. 13 elemental variants documented: 5 core Wuxing + 3 Metal splits + 5 extended (Cloud, Honey, Storm, Ice, Egg, Time).
